Popstar: Never Stop Never Stopping

This weekend I went to see Popstar: Never Stop Never Stopping. I thoroughly enjoyed it and would encourage anyone to see it. It also got favorable reviews for the most part per Rotten Tomatoes: PopStar Rotten Tomatoes Reviews

If you have never heard of the movie before, the script was written by Andy Samberg, Akiva Schaffer, and Jorma Taccone. These 3 guys make up the comedy trio "The Lonely Island", and are probably most well known for their digital shorts on Saturday Night Live . If interested, you can read more about "The Lonely Island" here: Lonely Island Wiki

The mockumentary revolves around Andy's character, megastar Conner4Real. Below is a list of a few other actors in the film and the roles they play: (source:Popstar Wiki)

  • Akiva Schaffer
      • Lawrence "Kid Brain" Dunn, Conner's former bandmate and songwriter
  • Jorma Taccone
      • Owen "Kid Contact" Bouchard, Conner's former bandmate and current DJ
  • Sarah Silverman
      • Paula Klein, his publicist
  • Tim Meadows
      • Harry Duggins, his manager
  • Chris Redd
      • Hunter the Hungry
